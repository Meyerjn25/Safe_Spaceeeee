Green Tea

The Chinese have long been using green tea to treat depression, but it might come as a surprise that green tea can also treat anxiety. Green tea comes with L-theanine, an amino acid that helps reduce stress and relaxes your mind and body. When going out to dinner, try swapping your water for a glass of green tea. You can also purchase green tea extract or L-theanine supplements. More Info.

Music Therapy

Music therapy can be used as a noninvasive method to reduce anxiety and depression. Studies conducted on patients with cancer who have expirenced music therapy have reported a reduction of anxiety and depressive symptoms. Music effects the brain through pitch, tempo, and dynamics and when we see these elements work together we see dramatic changes in physiology and behavior. More info.
